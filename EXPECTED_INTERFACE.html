üîß TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS

If you don't see the edit features in your Flask app, try these steps IN ORDER:

  1. Double-click on `restart_server.bat` to restart your Flask server
  2. Go to: http://localhost:5000/test-edit-features to run diagnostics
  3. Check that all items show "YES" in the diagnostic page
  4. Go to: http://localhost:5000/local-login
  5. Login with:
    • Engineering: eng123
    • Production: prod456
    • Administration: admin789
  6. Navigate to: http://localhost:5000/department-settings
  7. MAXIMIZE your browser window to see the 3-column layout
  8. Press Ctrl+F5 to force refresh and clear cache
  9. Look for:
    • Orange "Update Employee" button in the middle column
    • Orange "Update Shift" button in the middle column
    • Three forms per section instead of two
